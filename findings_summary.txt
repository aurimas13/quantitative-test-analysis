# Error Detection Summary

## Identified Issues:
1. **Price Anomalies**: Stocks with sudden jumps (>20%).
2. **Missing Trade Records**: Positions changing without trade records.
3. **Weight Calculation Errors**: Discrepancies between reported and computed weights.
4. **Trade Price Inconsistencies**: Trade prices not aligning with market prices.
5. **Currency Mismatches**: USD stocks using an incorrect exchange rate.
6. **Negative Holdings**: Positions showing unintended short selling.
7. **Market Cap Mismatches**: Incorrect company classification by market cap.

All detected errors have been saved in the `reports/` directory.
